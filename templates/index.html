About Our System

The Digital Ticket System for Public Transit aims to simplify and modernize your daily commute. Whether you're a student or a working professional, our platform offers an easy way to apply for bus or metro passes online. With real-time QR verification, secure data management, and tiered fare options, we’re committed to improving urban mobility for everyone.
